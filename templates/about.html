About PromptDB

PromptDB is a natural language-to-database interface. Our goal is to make interacting with your data as simple as having a conversation. Using AI, PromptDB translates plain English instructions into complex MongoDB commands, allowing you to find, insert, update, and delete data without writing a single line of code.

This project was built to demonstrate the power of large language models in automating complex tasks and bridging the gap between human language and technical operations.
